Operationalizing date offset:

 

Example: DATE OF PARTICIPANT ENROLLMENT

 

Breast patient – first positive biopsy date is 01/01/2018

Actual date of participant enrollment is 03/01/2018

 

The CDE being captured is “Date of participant enrollment number of days from anchor date”
Value for that CDE is 59

(where the anchor date is day 0)

 

Value of CDE can be positive or negative.
